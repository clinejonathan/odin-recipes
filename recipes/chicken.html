Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken, put flour in bag with seasonings
  2. Dip chicken in buttermilk, put chicken in bag, shake
  3. Place chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, let sit until flour becomes paste-like
  4. Fill skillet with 1/3-1/2 full of oil, turn up very hot
  5. Brown chicken on both sides, then turn down heat and cook for ~30 mins (until cooked through)
  6. raise heat again and crisp then let sit on paper towels until cool
